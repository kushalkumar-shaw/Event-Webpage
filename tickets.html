Is there a refund policy?

Full refunds are available until July 1, 2026. After that date, no refunds but transfers to another person are allowed.

Do tickets include accommodation?

No, but we have partnered hotels with special discounts. See our venue page for details.

Can I buy tickets at the door?

Yes, if available. However, online pre-registration is recommended and guarantees your seat.
