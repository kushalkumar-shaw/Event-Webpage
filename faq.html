Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tech Summit 2026

General Questions

What is Tech Summit 2026?

Tech Summit 2026 is a three-day annual conference bringing together tech professionals, entrepreneurs, and innovators. It features keynote presentations, technical workshops, networking opportunities, and exhibitions from leading tech companies.

When and where is the event?

Tech Summit 2026 takes place July 15-17, 2026 at the Moscone Convention Center in San Francisco. The venue is located at 747 Howard Street, SOMA district.

Who should attend?

The summit is ideal for software developers, system architects, tech entrepreneurs, product managers, CTOs, and anyone interested in the latest technology trends and innovations. Students and educators also benefit from special discounted rates.

How many people typically attend?

We expect over 5,000 attendees from around the world, with more than 50 expert speakers and 100+ sessions and workshops across three days.

Ticket & Registration

What ticket types are available?

We offer Standard ($99), Premium ($199), VIP ($399), Student ($49), and Corporate packages. Each tier provides different access levels and benefits. Check our Tickets page for detailed comparisons.

Is there early bird pricing?

Yes! We offer 20% discount on all tickets until June 30, 2026. Early registration is encouraged as slots are limited.

What's included in each ticket type?

All tickets include meals for three days, basic session access, and networking events. Premium and VIP tickets include additional benefits like workshop access, speaker meet-and-greets, and exclusive networking events. See our Tickets page for full details.

Can I change my ticket after purchase?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket up to 7 days before the event. For downgrades or other changes, please contact our support team.

What's your refund policy?

Full refunds are available until July 1, 2026. After that date, no refunds are offered, but you can transfer your ticket to another person at no additional charge.

Can I purchase tickets at the door?

Limited walk-up registration may be available if tickets remain unsold. However, we strongly recommend pre-registering online to guarantee your seat and avoid disappointment.

Do you offer corporate group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 10 or more receive special pricing and package options. Contact our sales team for a custom quote tailored to your organization.

Schedule & Sessions

What's the daily schedule?

Each day runs from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM with registration at 8 AM, keynotes from 9-10:30 AM, and concurrent sessions throughout the day with lunch and breaks. See our Schedule page for detailed timing.

Will I be able to attend all sessions?

Some sessions run concurrently, so you'll need to choose which ones to attend. We recommend planning your schedule in advance. Digital recordings will be available after the event for all sessions.

Are sessions recorded?

Yes, all main sessions and keynotes are recorded. Premium and VIP ticket holders get immediate access, while Standard ticket holders receive access after the event concludes.

What topics will be covered?

Topics include Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Architecture, Cybersecurity, DevOps, Web Development, Data Science, Entrepreneurship, and more. Check our Schedule page for the full list of sessions.

Will there be networking opportunities?

Absolutely! We have structured networking events each evening, plus informal networking during meals and breaks. VIP ticket holders get access to exclusive networking sessions.

Venue & Logistics

Where is the venue located?

Moscone Convention Center, 747 Howard Street, San Francisco, CA 94103. It's in the SOMA district, easily accessible by BART, Muni, or ride-sharing services.

How do I get to the venue?

You can take BART (Civic Center Station), Muni buses, drive your own car, or use ride-sharing. Parking is available at the venue ($25/day) or nearby garages. See our Venue page for detailed directions.

Is the venue accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, the Moscone Convention Center is fully accessible with wheelchair access, accessible restrooms, and accommodations for attendees with disabilities. Please contact us if you need specific accommodations.

Where can I stay?

We've partnered with several hotels near the venue offering special rates. Options include Hotel Nikko, InterContinental, Marriott Marquis, and St. Regis. Check our Venue page for details and booking links.

Is WiFi available at the venue?

Yes, high-speed WiFi is available throughout the venue at no additional cost. We also have dedicated work areas with power outlets and charging stations.

What's the parking situation?

Parking is available at the venue ($25/day) and in nearby garages. We recommend using public transportation or ride-sharing as parking may be limited. See our Venue page for more details.

Technical & Other

Do I need any special equipment or software?

No special equipment is required to attend. For workshops, basic programming knowledge and a laptop are recommended. Free WiFi is available throughout the venue.

Can I bring a guest?

Each ticket is for one person. If you want to bring additional guests, they'll need their own tickets. Group discounts are available for 10+ attendees.

Is this event suitable for beginners?

Yes! We have sessions at all levels from beginner to advanced. The Standard ticket provides good foundational content, while Premium and VIP tickets offer advanced workshops.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your ticket confirmation (digital or printed), ID, laptop (if attending workshops), business cards for networking, and comfortable shoes. Note-taking materials and a power bank are also helpful.
